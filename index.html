About

Our augmented reality scrapbooking platform allows users to document, organize, annotate, and share their memories with others in a safe and interactive way.

It offers a unique approach to scrapbooking by integrating and overlaying real-world content, and includes various community interaction aspects, such as sharing media related to a physical location, leaving messages and reactions, and derivative annotations to content.

The platform caters to different types of users and provides various interfaces for visualizing content, while also ensuring appropriate moderation and a safe environment. Join our community and start creating and sharing your memories in a new and exciting way.
